About the heat house project

The heat house projects is a project designed to demonstrate to students insulation in houses. The heat house projects end goal is to have each group create a properly insulated model house. The materials for creating these houses will be selected randomly through a draw. This website is dedicated to tell you about the challenges group #8 faced as well as how group #8 completed the heat house project.

Day number One

Date: 4/17/2017

The first challenge was to create the cut list for our heat house. This at first seemed simple but there was a twist group #8 could not use a ruler group #8 had to figure it out with the sheet provided. This sheet did not list all the measurments. Therefore group #8 had to compare certain peices to each other and do some math calculations. Once we had finished that our final cut list turned out to be 1X31cm,4X25cm,4X15cm,2X5cm and 4X12cm.
